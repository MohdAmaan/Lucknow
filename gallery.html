The residential colony inside which the Butler Palace is situated gets its name from the palace itself. The foundation stone was laid in 1915 by Raja Mehmoodabad.

The Chattar Manzil, or Umbrella Palace is a building in Lucknow in Uttar Pradesh which served as a palace for the rulers of Awadh and their wives.

RAMAKRISHNA MATH

A monastery and shrine, the Ramakrishna Math is an all-monk institution dedicated to Sri Ramakrishna.With the motto of "For one's own salvation, and for the welfare of the world".

Hazratganj

SHAH NAJAF IMAMBARA

The dome-shaped monument houses the graves of Nawab Ghazi-Ud-Din Haider.Adorning architecture amid a beautiful front floral yard, it is an all-white marble finished structure housing a large dome.

Qaiserbagh

SHAHEED SMARAK

Shaheed Smarak or Martyrs' Memorial is a white marbled pillar-like structure standing amidst lush green lawns Qaiserbagh. Established in memory of the soldiers who lost their lives during the 1857 War of Indian Independence.

Hazratganj

TOMBS OF SAADAT ALI KHAN

The Tomb of Saadat Ali Khan is a combined mausoleum of both Saadat Ali Khan and his wife Khursheed Zadi. Constructed by their son, this is a tomb adorning the typical amalgam of Indian and Islamic architectural styles.

Husainabad

SHAHI BAOLI

An intricate merge of Indian and Islamic architectural styles, the Shahi Baoli was formerly built as a reservoir for the society of the time. It was constructed between 1784 and 1795 by Nawab Asif-Ud-Din Daula and Kifayat-Ullah.

Scorpio Club or Scorpio Water Park is the perfect way to beat the summer heat amidst cool blue waters.You can get your adrenaline all rushed up with all the adventurous rides.The property also has a fine dine restaurant to satiate your hunger pangs.

NILANSH WATER PARK

Nilansh Theme Park hosts an array of fun games, activities and an exclusive water park. The site also avails space for events, private parties and gatherings and overnight stays.It is also UP's first day and night water park, so get set to make evening plans here as well
